Our Story

Building the intelligence layer for GCC enterprise

We started with a simple observation: businesses in the Gulf were running on ERP systems that didn't speak their language — not Arabic, not VAT, not ZATCA, not the reality of multi-entity operations across borders. Mumtaz was built to fix that.

Why we exist

ERP built for this region, not retrofitted to it

The GCC is one of the world's fastest-growing economic zones. It deserves software that's native to its regulatory environment, languages, and business patterns.

🎯

Mission

Empower GCC and South Asian businesses with ERP that thinks alongside them — surfacing insights before they're asked, flagging risks before they surface, and automating the workflows that drain teams.

⚖️

Compliance-first

ZATCA Phase 2 e-invoicing, UAE VAT, Saudi VAT, withholding tax — built in from day one, not bolted on. Our compliance layer updates automatically when regulations change.

🤝

Partner model

Banks, free zones, and chambers of commerce can white-label the full Mumtaz platform, earning recurring revenue while their members get world-class ERP at zero deployment friction.

Our journey

From idea to infrastructure

2022

Founded in Dubai

Team of ERP architects, ex-bankers, and AI engineers came together after recognising a gap in locally-built, regulation-aware business software for the Gulf.

2023

First enterprise deployments

Launched multi-entity ERP with full Arabic localisation and ZATCA Phase 1 e-invoicing across Saudi Arabia and UAE. First 50 business customers onboarded.

2024

ZAKI AI + bank partnerships

Introduced ZAKI, the conversational AI agent embedded in ERP. Signed first white-label agreements with regional banks and free zone authorities.

2025

B2B Marketplace & embedded finance

Launched the Mumtaz B2B Marketplace connecting buyers and suppliers with financing embedded at the point of purchase. Expanded to Pakistan and Qatar.

2026

Multi-country SaaS platform

Full multi-tenant SaaS with partner control plane, per-tenant database isolation, and full ZATCA Phase 2 clearance. Now serving 7 countries.
