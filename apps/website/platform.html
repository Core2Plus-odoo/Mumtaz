Platform pillars

Built for the way GCC businesses actually operate

Every design decision was made with multi-currency, multi-entity, multi-country Gulf businesses in mind.

01

Multi-tenant SaaS

Each business gets a fully isolated database with its own schema, user directory, and configuration. No shared tables, no data bleed between tenants.

02

GCC-native compliance

ZATCA Phase 2, UAE VAT, Saudi WHT, Corporate Tax — all built into the platform and maintained as regulations evolve. No third-party compliance plugins needed.

03

Arabic-first UI

Full right-to-left interface, Arabic document templates, Arabic chart of accounts presets, and bilingual email/SMS communications.

04

White-label partner mode

Banks and free zones can deploy Mumtaz under their own brand — custom domain, logo, and pricing. Full control plane for managing tenants and billing.

05

AI embedded by default

ZAKI AI is not a bolt-on. It reads every record in your ERP in real time, so answers are always current — not based on stale snapshots or exports.

06

Open API

Full REST API with JWT auth, rate-limited by plan, with webhook support for all entity events. Connect any external tool, BI platform, or custom workflow.
