Setup checklist

Complete these steps to go from zero to fully ZATCA-compliant. The QR generator above works without onboarding — submission requires steps 3 & 4.

  1. Set ZATCA_SELLER_NAME and ZATCA_VAT_NUMBER on the server
  2. Choose environment: ZATCA_ENV=sandbox for testing, production when live
  3. Generate a CSR + load ZATCA_PRIVATE_KEY
  4. Get an OTP from the Fatoora portal and POST it to /api/zatca/onboard
  5. Configured ZATCA_CSID — invoices can now clear / report
Heads-up. The ZATCA Phase 2 clearance and reporting flows are scaffolded — we generate compliant QR codes today, but invoice signing & submission to ZATCA still require production credentials. Talk to hello@mumtaz.digital to enable full clearance for your tenant.
