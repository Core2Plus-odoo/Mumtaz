FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

What's included in the 14-day free trial?
You get full access to all Growth plan features during your trial — including AI CFO (Zaki), cash flow forecasting, and finance connectivity. No credit card required to start.
Can I change my plan after signing up?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. If you upgrade, you're prorated immediately. If you downgrade, the change takes effect at your next billing cycle.
How does the annual billing discount work?
Choosing annual billing saves you 20% compared to paying month-to-month. You'll be charged once per year. You can switch to annual billing at any time.
What does "users" mean? Are there read-only roles?
A "user" is anyone with a named login who can create or edit records. We offer read-only viewer roles that don't count toward your user limit — useful for executives, auditors, or bank partners reviewing data.
I'm a bank or fintech — which plan is right for us?
Financial institutions typically need the Enterprise plan for partner API access, white-label options, and dedicated integration support. Contact our partnerships team at partnerships@mumtaz.digital for a tailored conversation.
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We operate on SOC 2 compliant infrastructure and perform regular security audits. Enterprise plans include options for private cloud or on-premise deployment.
What happens if I exceed my user limit?
We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit. You can upgrade your plan at any time without losing any data or settings. We don't hard-block access — we work with you to find the right solution.
