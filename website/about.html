Our Mission

Building stronger SME ecosystems.

SMEs are the backbone of regional economies. Yet most lack access to the operational intelligence and financial infrastructure that larger businesses take for granted. Mumtaz is changing that.

Why We Exist

The SME gap is real

Across the GCC and MENA region, SMEs contribute over 50% of GDP and employ the majority of the private sector workforce. Yet they are consistently underserved by the tools and financial infrastructure available to them.

Mumtaz was built to close that gap — by giving every SME access to the operating intelligence, AI guidance, and financial connectivity that was previously only available to large enterprises.
